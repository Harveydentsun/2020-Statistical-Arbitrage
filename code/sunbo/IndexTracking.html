问题一：已知股票池，确定权重¶

Nnn<N

  1. $M$
    $\tilde{r}_m$
    $\sigma_m^2=var(\tilde{r}_m)$
  2. $n$ $\tilde{r}_1,\tilde{r}_2,...\tilde{r}_n$
    $\mu_j=E(\tilde{r}_j)$ $\mu = (\mu_1,\mu_2,...\mu_n)^T$
    $\sigma_j^2 = var(\tilde{r}_j)$
    $\sigma_{ij}=cov(\tilde{r}_i,\tilde{r}_j)$ $\Sigma$
  3. $n$$x_1,x_2,...x_n$$\sum_{j=1}^n{x_j}=1$$x = (x_1,x_2,...x_n)$
    $E(\tilde{r}_p)=\sum_{i=j}^n{x_j\tilde{r}_j}$
  4. $\mu_m=E(\tilde{r}_m)$
    $\sigma_{jM}=cov(\tilde{r}_j,\tilde{r}_m)$
    Beta $\beta_j=\frac{\sigma_{jM}}{\sigma_m^2}$$\beta = (\beta_1,\beta_2,...\beta_n)$

问题三：一般问题¶

Nnn<N

  1. $\tilde{r}_t=(\tilde{r}_t^1,\tilde{r}_t^2,...\tilde{r}_t^N)^T$ $t$$\tilde{r}_t^m$$t$
  2. $w=(w_1,w_2,...w_N)^T$ $w_i=0$ $i$
    $t$$\tilde{r}_t^p(w)=\tilde{r}_t^T w$
  3. $0<\epsilon_i<\xi_i<1$ i $$\epsilon_i y_i \leq w_i \leq \xi_i y_i $$
    $$\text{ } y_i=\left\{\begin{aligned}1, & \text{ if } w_i>0 \\0, & \text{ else }\end{aligned}\right.$$
    $$\text{ }L \leq \sum_{i=1}^N y_i \leq K$$
  4. $\bar{w}_i$ : $$\sum_{i=1}^N |w_i-\bar{w}_i| \leq TO$$ $\gamma_i =|w_i-\bar{w}_i|$ , $$\sum_{i=1}^N \gamma_i \leq TO$$ $$- \gamma_i \leq w_i-\bar{w}_i \leq \gamma_i$$

$$\begin{align*} \min_{w_i,y_i,\gamma_i} \ & f(w) = \sqrt{\frac{1}{T} \sum_{t=1}^T (\tilde{r}_t^p(w)-\tilde{r}_t^m)^2} \\ \mbox{s.t. } & \epsilon_i y_i \leq w_i \leq \xi_i y_i \\ & L \leq \sum_{i=1}^N y_i \leq K\\ & y_i \in \{0,1\} \; \forall i \\ & \sum_{i=1}^N \gamma_i \leq TO \\ & - \gamma_i \leq w_i-\bar{w}_i \leq \gamma_i \end{align*}$$
